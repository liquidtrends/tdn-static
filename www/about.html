About Us

TDN Contracting Ltd is a Chipewyan Prairie First Nation totality owned company, which operates out of Janvier, AB. We encourage hiring local community members for work first.

We have completed our Safety external audit in 2015 and are fully qualified core with the Alberta Construction Safety Association (CORS). We have also recently launched our Quality Manual. In addition, we are a member of ISNetworld and can provide our identification number when requested.

TDN Contracting Ltd. is a wholly owned Chipewyan Prairie Dene First Nation company.
